Moderator Application

Application Requirements

To be accepted as a moderator, you must:

  1. Hold at least 20,000 NUUR tokens in your wallet
  2. Tokens must be bought from the presale (not direct transfers from another address)
  3. Provide accurate contact information

After submission, your application will be reviewed by the NUUR team. We will verify your token holdings and presale participation.
